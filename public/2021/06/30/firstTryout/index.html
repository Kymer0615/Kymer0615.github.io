First Tryout

Posted on 2021-06-30 Edited on 2021-07-05 In 涂鸦 Graffiti Views: Valine:


This photo was taken in 15 May 2020 at 17:42:00 while I and Gab were waiting for our friend Rabbit, to pick up some sandwiches outside of a mall. By the time, we already had the idea to do graffiti because we found so many T-ups and tags in the town when we were hanging around the city during night times.

However, the idea was firstly originated from the notorious Banksy’s auction event in 2018; we had plans to do some stencils(some big gold fish) on the streets just like Banksy’s works, on the day before my trip to Japan in 2019, and – shit happened; I twisted my ankle on that day playing basketball :( so the plan was postponed to the next summer.

The word on that sticker is PLUS(our current crew name), a substitution for sandwiches, and it is an interesting word since the Chinese pronunciation of it(加 Jiā) is similar to other words like “home” in Chinese or “Jah” in English. I was the one who put the sticker on the steel pipe and you can tell it was not positioned properly because I was a bit nervous.

From that day on, Gab bought some tools like dabbers from online because we were so influenced by the videos on Youtube from Wekman aka “tag machine”. And, at the beginning, we were not prepared to write on the street so we tried those tools in a basketball court.

  • I believe this is one of the first graffiti photos that I took in the town⬆.
    We were so dumb to use dabbers on the concrete floor and those dabbers were just ruined. But, at the time, we thought those tags looked so cool and I believe I definitely had a good sleep :)
